Hello, I'm Ayoub !
  I'm a novice teacher of the English language. I am teaching and supporting learners for more than a year, and I can proudly say that until now, I learned a lot concerning the field of teaching. There are so many obstacles or rather I would say "challenges" that face language learners. Especially, those who learn it as a foreign language. I wouldn't deny that I have suffered as a student. The issues were highly related to the lack of intrinsic motivation. I wasn't a courageous figure. Thus, for being an introvert, I had to make a major effort to overpass this state in my personality.
  I am offering help and continuous support to learners ,who are like me, willing  to improve . In fact, I am willing to change what I have to change in order to make 'altogether' the world a beautiful and peaceful place to live in.

About Me
